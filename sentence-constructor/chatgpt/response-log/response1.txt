Student input: The dog would not stop barking, did you feed him?


Response:

To transcribe "The dog would not stop barking, did you feed him?" into Japanese, here's how you can do it:

**犬は吠え続けることを止めなかった、彼に餌をあげましたか？**  
(*Inu wa hoe tsuzukeru koto o tomenakatta, kare ni esa o agemashita ka?*)

Here’s a breakdown:
- 犬 (いぬ, *inu*) = dog
- は (wa) = topic marker
- 吠え続ける (ほえつづける, *hoe tsuzukeru*) = to continue barking
- ことを止めなかった (ことをやめなかった, *koto o tomenakatta*) = did not stop (verb + koto = the action of barking)
- 彼に (かれに, *kare ni*) = to him (referring to the dog as male)
- 餌をあげましたか (えさをあげましたか, *esa o agemashita ka?*) = did you feed him?
